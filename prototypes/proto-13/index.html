Privacy by default

Investigative journalism has a threat model. Mycroft is built to match it.

ZDR providers, local-first mode, no telemetry. Your vault stays on disk. Your keys stay in your browser. The setup form has no backend.

  • Zero Data RetentionOnly ZDR providers (Fireworks, Together) ship as defaults. No Claude, OpenAI, or Gemini.
  • Local-first modeOne toggle and every inference runs on your machine. Zero egress. $0/month.
  • Your keys, your machineThe setup page runs in your browser. API keys embed in the .command you download.
  • Firecrawl only for the webWeb fetching uses Firecrawl exclusively — no surveillance-adjacent scraping middlemen.
  • Your vault stays on diskFindings write to your Obsidian vault. Nothing syncs to our infrastructure.
  • No telemetryNo analytics, no crash reporting, no phone home. Open-source; read every line.
