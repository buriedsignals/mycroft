About Mycroft

Most AI tools are generalists — chatbots trained to be agreeable. Mycroft is opinionated. It runs on open-weight models, keeps your sources on disk, and is built specifically for investigative methodology.

Plugins

What ships with Mycroft

2026at launch

Spotlight

OSINT investigation system. Multi-phase research, adversarial SIFT fact-checking, evidence grounding, knowledge-vault ingestion.

2026at launch

coJournalist

Beat-monitoring engine. Scheduled scouts, change detection, relevance scoring. Web-hosted runs 24/7; self-host option available.

2026May 2026

DataHound

Government and public-data API discovery with automated monitoring. Turn obscure open-data endpoints into queryable beats.

2026May 2026

Atelier

Visual production — charts, maps, video, infographics, social cards. Data and narrative to publication-ready visuals.
