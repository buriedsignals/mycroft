# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Positive behaviour includes:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologising to those affected by our mistakes
- Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the community

Unacceptable behaviour includes:

- Sexualised language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information — physical, electronic, or source-related — without explicit permission
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

**Journalism-specific:** given the audience, we treat any leak or doxing of a user's confidential sources, unpublished investigations, or whistleblower contacts as maximally unacceptable conduct.

## Enforcement

Reports go to: buriedsignals@agentmail.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Project maintainers are obliged to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.
