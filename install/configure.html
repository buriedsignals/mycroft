01 — Sovereignty

Where the work happens.

Pick one. Cloud is fast and affordable — ZDR open-weight providers in the US/EU. Local is yours, fully — a fine-tuned Qwen on your machine. Whichever you choose is the default; the other side stays available as fallback.

02 — Local model

Local models.

Pick the model that matches your hardware. The 9B is the Mycroft daily driver and fits any 16 GB+ machine; the 27B needs 32 GB+ unified memory. The installer downloads it into ~/models/ and registers it with Goose's built-in local inference.

Browser-only hardware probe. No data leaves this page.
